Summer 2025 · Seoul

Maximizing Exposure

Going into the summer of 2025, I was incredibly, unbearably restless. Maybe it was being in the suburbs for school when I have lived in the city so long, maybe it was that I was starting to lose my love for math which I had originally enjoyed. But I was so restless.

So when I was given two different offers for internships that summer — one remote but in EST, the other in person in Seoul — I took both.

At Socialladder, a marketing SaaS company that has worked with brands like medicube, Lululemon, and Beatbox, I worked as a CSM and UX research intern, which taught me a lot about how to navigate products and improve it and experience it from the client perspective. I also got to dip my toes in web growth, data analytics, and storytelling marketing there.

At VESSL AI, an AI Infrastructure & MLOps company, I almost exclusively worked on digital growth strategy and content. Here we were able to make a significant increase in website traffic through website auditing & SEO, and I also developed the skills to organize analytics tracking.

At Yonsei University, I took a computer graphics class where we used mostly Adobe programs to learn various forms of design.

So my schedule looked like this:

  • Wake up & finish up work for Socialladder
  • Go to Yonsei and take class
  • Head to VESSL AI office while eating lunch
  • VESSL AI work
  • Home → dinner & workout if time
  • Socialladder work begins & lasts until sleep

It was a crazy and hectic summer, but I was so locked in. I learned so many skills and gained so much perspective and experience.
